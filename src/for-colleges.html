FAQs

Is the content safe for faculty and management?

Yes. We keep it clean, campus-friendly, and avoid religion, politics, and explicit content. Sets can be pre-aligned with your faculty coordinator if needed.

What tech do we need to arrange?

Basic sound system (2 mics + speakers) and stage lighting is ideal. If you don't have it sorted, we can share a simple tech rider or connect you to vendors.

Do you travel outside Pune?

Yes. We travel for fests and flagship events. Travel and stay are either arranged by the college or added to the quote.

Can we record the show for our aftermovie?

Yes, with prior consent. We can either let your team record or bring our own video crew as an add-on.

Plan your next college event with us.

Share your fest dates, rough budget, and expected crowd. We’ll suggest the right format—freshers icebreaker, fest headliner, or a chill department show.
