About Laugh & Vibe

A creative playground for comedy, music, and unforgettable moments. We blend entertainment and connection to create experiences that feel alive.

Our Story

Laugh & Vibe started with a simple idea: create a space where people can walk in stressed and walk out lighter. From indie shows in cafés to a full-fledged 50-seater studio in Koregaon Park, we’ve grown into a home for artists, creators, and audiences who love live performance.

Today, we host weekend comedy shows, corporate entertainment, music gigs, and private events — keeping the vibe authentic, welcoming, and full of energy.

What We Stand For

Community
